Hygieia

Hygea is an innovative waste management solution leveraging automation, artificial intelligence, and image processing to revolutionize recycling practices. Inspired by the Greek goddess of health and cleanliness, Hygea aims to address global waste management challenges, such as inefficient recycling practices and overflowing bins, by streamlining waste sorting and management. The system utilizes advanced tools like Arduino microcontrollers, ultrasonic sensors, and OpenCV-based image processing to identify and sort recyclable materials effectively. With applications ranging from household recycling to community programs, Hygea promotes environmental sustainability and resource conservation, paving the way for a cleaner, greener future.
